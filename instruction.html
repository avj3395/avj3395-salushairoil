SALU’S SAMRUDHA KESHA herbal hair oil is not a regular hair oil but an Herbal hair oil for dandruff and hair fall. Apply alternately or weekly twice on the scalp two Hrs before bath and massage well & cover with hair cap. Wash off excess oil using mild shampoo or green gram powder, chemparathi thali and avoid hot water. We can hold the oil over night and wash off in the morning. If you are using first time please avoid (Neruka) only for the first time.

APPLY OIL GENTLY ALL OVER THE SCALP. RUB YOUR SCALP WITH YOUR NAILS. FOCUS & RUB OIL OVER HAIR LOSS AREAS

MASSAGE YOUR SCALP FOR ABOUT 15 MINUTES, SO THAT THE OIL IS FULLY ABSORBED INTO SCALP

LEAVE FOR 45 MINUTES TO 1 HOUR TIME. LET THE SCALP ABSORB APPLIED OIL

AFTER 45 MINUTES, WASH YOUR HAIR AND SCALP WITH WATER . DON’T USE ANY SHAMPOO OR ANY OTHER SOLUTION. DON’T USE HOT WATER.

AFTER WASHING, YOUR SCALP WILL REMAIN WITH OIL LAYER. JUST DRY YOUR HAIR GENTLY WITH TOWEL. DON’T DRY COMPLETELY. LET THE SCALP BE IN HALF WET CONDITION
